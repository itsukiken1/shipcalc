# ShipCalc - Shipping Cost from China Calculator

**[Use it free → itsukiken1.github.io/shipcalc](https://itsukiken1.github.io/shipcalc/)**

Instantly estimate shipping costs from China to 15+ countries. Compare sea freight, air freight, express courier, and rail rates.

## Features

- **6 origin ports**: Shenzhen, Yiwu, Shanghai, Ningbo, Qingdao, Xiamen
- **15 destination countries**: US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, Australia, Canada, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, South Korea, Brazil, Mexico
- **5 shipping methods**: Sea FCL, Sea LCL, Air Standard, Express Courier (DHL/FedEx/UPS), China-Europe Rail
- Volumetric weight calculation
- Goods type surcharges (electronics, hazmat, cosmetics, etc.)
- Side-by-side comparison with cost/speed ranking
- Smart shipping tips per destination

## Why?

Importers and cross-border sellers need quick shipping cost estimates when evaluating products from China. Most freight calculators require signup or are overly complex. ShipCalc gives you instant estimates with zero friction.

## Privacy

- No data collection
- No signup required
- All calculations run locally in your browser
